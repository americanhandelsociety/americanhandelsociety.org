Join the society

Become a member of the American Handel Society: stay up-to-date on cutting edge research and gain access to international Handel scholars, performers, and connoisseurs. Specific benefits of membership include:

  • A subscription to the Newsletter of the American Handel Society, published three times a year, which contains reports on scholarly developments, notices of upcoming conferences and performances, reviews, lists of recent publications, and news of the society;
  • Assistance in becoming a member of the Georg-Friedrich-Händel-Gesellschaft, Halle, and receiving the Händel-Jahrbuch;
  • Assistance in becoming a Friend of The Handel Institute (Great Britain), and receiving The Handel Institute Newsletter.

To join or renew your membership online, please select the appropriate class of membership from the drop-down list below, click "add to cart," and complete the payment process.
