Newsletter

The Newsletter is published three times a year (Spring, Summer, Winter) and contains reports on scholarly developments, notices of upcoming conferences and performances, reviews of books and recordings, an annual discography, lists of recent publications, and news of the Society.

Minji Kim, Editor
minjik@gmail.com

The Editor welcomes comments and suggestions for future issues.

Special Offers from the American Handel Society

The American Handel Society is offering sets of back issues of the Newsletter for the price of $10 per year (three issues each), going back to the first year, 1986. All volumes are available, but some numbers are in short supply. We reserve the right to supply photocopies of individual numbers where necessary.
