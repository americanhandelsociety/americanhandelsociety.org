Make a donation

The American Handel Society accepts donations in support of the following five categories:

  • Conference travel grants to assist graduate students and independent scholars
  • Support for Howard Serwer Memorial Lecture
  • Support for J. Merrill Knapp Research Fellowship
  • Support for Paul Traver Memorial Concert
  • Support for ongoing activities of the Society

If you wish to contribute to these causes, you may do so by clicking the "Donate Now" button below. Please specify the category or categories you wish to support by entering a message during checkout.





You can also make a donation by mail. Please download and complete the application form and send it to the appropriate address as indicated.
